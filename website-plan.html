GameVerse Finder – Site Plan

Site Name

GameVerse Finder

I picked this name because it’s simple and it kinda explains what the site does which is helping people find and explore different video games easily.

Optional domain: gameversefinder.com (just an example)

Site Purpose

The site helps users explore different video games by showing details like title, genre, rating, release year, and cover art. It will also have a contact page where users can reach out to the site owner, and a home page explaining the website’s goal.

Scenarios

  • What are the top rated games in a specific genre?
  • How can I contact the owner or get help with something on the site?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Sea Green – #40916c Used for headings and important elements.

Secondary Color: Mint Leaf – #74c69d Used for borders, highlights, accents.

Accent Color: Hunter Green – #2d6a4f Used for strong emphasis like bold labels or section lines.

Background Tint: Frosted Mint – #d8f3dc Used as the main background to keep things soft and clean.

Text Color: Carbon Black – #081c15 Used for normal text.

Typography

Roboto – for body text, paragraphs, and general content.

Cinzel Decorative – for headings, titles, and section headers to make them stand out.
